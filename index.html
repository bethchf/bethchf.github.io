Beth Chan

bethchf@u.nus.edu; curriculum vitae

I’m a Master’s student (English Language and Linguistics — Research) at the Department of English, Linguistics and Theatre Studies, National University of Singapore. My research interests include syntax and semantics, and investigating (grammatical) patterns in Singapore English using experimental methods and corpus data. I received my BA in Linguistics at the National University of Singapore under the supervision of Zheng Shen.
